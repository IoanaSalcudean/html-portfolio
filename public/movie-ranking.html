The Best Movies According to Ioana

My top 3 movies.

Zootopia

“Zootopia,” a fantasy set in a city where predators and prey live together in harmony, is a funny, beautifully designed kids’ film

Nea Marin Miliardar

Uncle Marin, the Billionaire (Romanian: Nea Mărin miliardar) is a 1979 Romanian comedy film directed by Sergiu Nicolaescu. Nea Mărin miliardar, ranks 1st in the top of the most watched Romanian films of all time, as attested by a 2006 press release of the Union of Authors and Film Makers of Romania.

The Impossible

"The Impossible"(2012) is an emotionally resonant film based on the incredible true story of a family's survival in the 2004 tsunami in Thailand.
